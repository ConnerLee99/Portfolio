This is a bit about me. I am a creative person by nature and I love to design. I am an avid musician who loves to play guitar. Towards the end of the program we had a careers day where I was first introduced to Code Nation. The idea of going back to coding after a few years really interested me. After starting the course with Code Nation I now know that becoming a developer is a definitely a career I want to pursue. It was tough considering we were all relatively new to coding but after a lot of trial and error we finally got a working game that met our goals when we started. I enjoy coding because it gives me a chance to be creative in a different way than graphics ever could have.
